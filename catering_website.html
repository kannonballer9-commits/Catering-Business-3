Meet the Chef

Hi, I’m Kannon Christiansen — a 20-year-old college student from Chico, CA with a passion for cooking. What started in my kitchen for friends has grown into meals for events, big and small. Since I was a kid, I’ve loved experimenting in the kitchen and creating meals that make people smile.

Our Food Philosophy

I don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all menu. Every event is unique, and I work with you to build a menu that fits your taste, budget, and style. From casual comfort food to elegant multi-course dinners, we’ll design something together that makes your occasion unforgettable. From hotdogs and hamburgers at the backyard barbeque to multiple course meals at weddings, I cover it all.

Rather than a fixed list, think of this as a conversation. Want grazing boards and finger foods for a casual get‑together? Done. Need hearty entrées for a family‑style meal? Covered. Looking for a sweet finish to cap the night? I’ll make it happen. Whatever your event calls for, we’ll create the perfect spread together.

This menu is a starting point — not a rulebook. We’ll collaborate to make it yours.
